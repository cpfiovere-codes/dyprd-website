01 — Introduction

Who we are.
Why it matters.

DYP is the global reference network for Dominican professional leadership. Our brand communicates institutional authority, cultural pride, and the ambition of a generation ready to lead at every level — from Washington Heights to the United Nations.

Empower Dominican professionals in the diaspora through leadership formation, institutional representation, and community action.

V

Be the global reference network for Dominican leadership — connecting talent, elevating voices, and building institutions that last.

P
Promise

Every Dominican professional deserves a seat at the table. DYP builds the table.

06 — Usage Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts.

The DYP brand is an institutional asset. Protect it with the same care we give our community.

✓   Always Do
✓Use the official shield on all institutional communications
✓Keep the gold border intact — never remove it from the shield
✓Use Playfair Display for all display headlines
✓Maintain sufficient contrast — minimum 4.5:1 ratio
✓Use bilingual copy whenever the audience is mixed
✓Write "Dominican Young Professionals" in full on first use
✕   Never Do
✕Stretch, rotate, or distort the shield
✕Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
✕Use unofficial colors or alter the palette
✕Use all-lowercase for "DYP" — it is always capitalized
✕Combine the DYP logo with other organization logos without approval
✕Use generic fonts like Arial or Times New Roman
Clear Space Rule

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "D" in "DYP" around all sides of the logo. Never crowd it.

07 — Applications

DYP in the world.

How the brand comes alive across touchpoints — digital, print, and in person.

01
Website & Digital
The primary digital home. DYP blue background with gold accents. Bilingual navigation. AI Guardian assistant for 24/7 engagement.
02
Business Cards
Navy front with gold shield and name in Playfair. White back with contact info in Montserrat. Printed on 16pt matte stock.
03
Letterhead
Navy header bar, shield top-left. Cormorant Garamond body text. Gold rule below header. Used for all official correspondence.
04
Social Media
Profile: Shield on navy. Posts use the 3-color grid system (navy · white · red). Always bilingual captions. @dominicanyp.
05
Event Signage
Large-format banners: Shield centered on navy. Event name in Playfair Display at 72pt+. Tricolor accent bar at base.
06
Certificates & Awards
Cream background. Shield at top-center. Recipient name in Playfair Display italic. Gold border. Signatures in blue ink.
